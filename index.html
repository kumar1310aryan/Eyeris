WHO WE ARE

Through powerful visual storytelling, hands-on workshops, impactful campaigns and responsible journalism, Eyeris supports changemakers across the development spectrum in showcasing their impact to the world-to witness, appreciate and emulate.

With the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) as our blueprint, we create Development Insights for governments, corporations, NGOs, academia, civil society, grassroots communities, and others who share our commitment to making the world a better place.

WHAT WE DO

With the goal of empowering organizations, programmes, and individuals working towards the Sustainable Development
Goals(SDGs), we offer four key services:

Filmmaking

Filmmaking

As a factual film production company, Eyeris offers end-to-end filmmaking services across formats. From documentaries and explainers to animations and other forms of non-fiction video content, we craft compelling visual stories grounded in truth.

Immersive Media

Immerssive Media

While immersive filmmaking falls under our broader film work, we see it as a space with unique potentail-and a future USP for Eyeris. We are currently exploring the use of VR in wildlife and other critical development themes.

Workshops and Events

We believe in sharing what we know. Through workshops, campaigns, and community-driven events, we build capacities and create platforms that enable changemakers to amplify their work and impact.

Workshops and Events

Journalistic Services

Having started our journey in journalism, we continue to offer a range of editorial services-including reporting, editing, interviewing, and translation-with a strong focus on the environment and development sectors.
