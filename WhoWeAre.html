WHO WE ARE

We are an evolving collective medial professionals committed to driving progress in the development sector. We enable development programmes and orgainsations to showcase and scale therri impact through conventional as well as emerging forms of media.

Eyeris Media began with the editorial development of a newspaper in 2015, and has metamorphosed into a full-fledged storytelling journey across the medial landscape, spanning traditional formats such as films and graphics to modern immersive technologies like VR and AR.

Through powerful visual storytelling, hands-on workshops, impactful campaigns and responsible journalism, we support changemakers across the development spectrum in showcasing their impact to the world-to witness, appreciate and emulate.

With the Sustainable Development Goals(SDGs) as our blueprint, we collaborate with governments, corporations, NGOs, academia, civil society, grassroots communities, and others who share our commitment to making the world a better place.

What's in the name?

There's more to Eyeris than what meets the 'eye'.
The word Eyeris is a neologism derived from the iris of the eye. The eye is symbolic of everything that we do-create insights on social, economic, and environmental developments. The iris is the most visually defining part of the eye. Similarly, Eyeris aspires to define development through visual insights.

CrocEye, our logo

Our journey as filmmakers began with a documntary on reptile conservation, and the reptilian elements in our logo, including the macro shot of a crocodile's eye, the letterr 'i' traced off its pupil, and the discreet Ouroboros, honour these creatures.

The crocodile in the shot was filmed during a conservation project, wherein it was rescued from hostile conditionsn and released into a betterr environment.
Through its eyes, Eyeris aspires to show the world a betterr version of itself.
